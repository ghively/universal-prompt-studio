name: Plan-then-execute
lever: reasoning
helps: multi-constraint generation (satisfy 5 rules at once); non-reasoning models on complex tasks
hurts: reasoning models (redundant); latency/cost-sensitive calls
---
Ask for a short plan (2-4 bullets) before the deliverable, or split into two calls:
plan, then execute the approved plan.

**Mechanism**: committing to structure before prose prevents the model from writing
itself into a corner and abandoning constraints mid-generation.
